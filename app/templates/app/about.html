Family is a huge part of who I am. I wouldn't be anything that I am today if it wasn't for the influence of all of my family members. As the 4th of 5 children I appreciate the example that each one in addition to that my parents. They all have provided me with a lot of guidence to get me where I am today. In addition to my family, I grew up in far reaches of Maine and that is something that defines a big part of who I am.

I like to live by the motto: "Work hard, play hard", so whenever I'm not busy with school work or a paying job, I try to be doing the things that I love. I really enjoy being outside whenever I can, and being as active as I can be. Rock climbing, canyoneering, backpacking, and just travelling in general are some of my favorite things to do. Despite all of these trips, there are a lot of times where there is nothing better than sitting at home and watching a movie.
