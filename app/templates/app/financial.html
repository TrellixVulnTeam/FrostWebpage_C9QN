The basic purpose of this module is to see the effect that the way news is presented has upon the subject matter. In this study I have decided to follow several companies and compare the tone of news articles about them to their change in stock prices. In order to analyze the tone, or sentiment, that news articles have towards a company, I have used APIs and text analytics to create a sentiment score for these companies daily. That score ranges from 1 to -1. A positive number indicates a positive sentiment and a negative number indicates a negative sentiment. This is then graphed side-by-side with the opening and closing stock prices for that company on the same day.

The blue lines represent the stock prices over time. The solid line is the opening sales price, while the dashed line is the closing sales price. The red dots are the setiment scores for each day.
